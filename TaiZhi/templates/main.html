About Project

We are facing challenges in energy management and need smarter solutions to integrate agriculture with renewable energy. Agrivoltaics is an innovative microgrid system designed for recreational farms, aiming to improve energy efficiency and operational performance.

The system offers features like power generation forecasting, solar panel anomaly detection, and automated control. It uses advanced models to predict energy supply, monitors solar panel issues in real time to reduce maintenance costs, and employs LightController hardware to adjust lighting automatically based on ambient light conditions for energy savings.

Additionally, the system collects and analyzes operational data, presenting insights on a smart energy platform to support informed decision-making for farm operators. Agrivoltaics sets a new standard for smart agriculture and sustainable energy management.
